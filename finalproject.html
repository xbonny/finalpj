Sleep

The lack of sleep that the students face is one of the major health issues for students with half of all students reporting regularly reporting sleepiness during the day.Recent research on college students and sleep indicates that insufficient sleep impacts our health, our moods, our GPA, our safety, and motivation. Sleep really matters.Sleep is a necessity, yet college students give more importance on the courses they are taking to complete their degrees. College students are often at risk for having mental health issues such as depression and anxiety, and researchers believe that lack of sleep is a factor.

Not only college students are affected with the lack of sleep,but also high highschool students. High school students can also encounter the same negative impacts as college students. Having sleep deprivation can effect the growth of teens. Growth hormones normally are released during sleep. Not only the lack of sleep effect the growth hormones but other hormones as well. Also the lack of sleep can linked to obesity and diabetes.
